Our Story 我们的故事

A Bridge Between China's Medical Innovation and the World

连接中国医药创新与世界的桥梁

Shanghai Rolin Biotechnology Company Ltd. (Rolin Bio) is a Shanghai-based professional services firm dedicated to empowering Chinese pharmaceutical and medical device enterprises to succeed in international markets. Our core expertise spans overseas market research, local regulatory access, medical device and pharmaceutical import/export, and full-scale overseas commercialization.

上海锐灵生物科技有限公司（简称锐灵生物，英文名 Shanghai Rolin Biotechnology Company Ltd.，英文简称 Rolin Bio）是一家注册于上海、专注于为中国医药医疗企业提供出海服务的专业机构。公司核心业务涵盖海外市场调研、当地准入与法规服务、医疗器械及药品进出口，以及海外市场商业化等领域。

Our team possesses deep knowledge of local regulatory frameworks, market access pathways, and clinical practice patterns across target regions. We have successfully guided dozens of domestic medical device enterprises through the complete lifecycle—from product registration in high-value orthopedic implant segments (spine, trauma, sports medicine) to channel development and academic promotion—enabling our clients to transition seamlessly from product export to brand establishment.

团队熟悉当地法律法规、市场准入流程与临床使用习惯，已成功协助数十家国内医疗器械企业完成骨科植入耗材（含脊柱、创伤、运动医学等细分领域）在脊柱、创伤与运动医学等高值耗材领域的注册申报、渠道建设与学术推广全流程服务，助力客户实现从产品出海到品牌落地的跨越式发展。
