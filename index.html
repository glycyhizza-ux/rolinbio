Who We Are

Your Trusted Partner for Global Market Entry

Shanghai Rolin Biotechnology Co., Ltd. (Rolin Bio) is a Shanghai-based professional services firm dedicated to helping Chinese pharmaceutical and medical device enterprises succeed in international markets.

With deep expertise in regulatory affairs, market access strategy, and local commercialization across emerging markets—especially Southeast Asia and MENA—Rolin Bio has built robust local service networks. Our team has successfully guided dozens of domestic medical device companies through full-process market entry, from product registration and channel development to academic promotion and clinical adoption.

锐灵生物深耕海外医药市场，尤其擅长东南亚及中东北非地区的市场开拓。公司从业人员具备丰富的海外项目运营经验，熟稔当地法律法规，在东南亚与中东北非等新兴市场已建立本地化服务网络，可快速响应客户从产品准入到终端覆盖的全周期需求。已成功协助数十家国内医疗器械企业完成骨科植入耗材、脊柱、创伤与运动医学等高值耗材领域的注册申报、渠道建设与学术推广全流程服务。
