## Vietnam's Revised Pharmaceutical Law: A New Era

Vietnam's National Assembly has passed the revised Pharmaceutical Law, set to take effect on January 1, 2027. The legislation represents the most significant regulatory overhaul in over a decade and creates both opportunities and new requirements for foreign pharmaceutical manufacturers.

### Key Provisions

1. **GMP Recognition Expansion:** Vietnam will expand recognition of foreign GMP certifications, including those from PIC/S member countries. This reduces duplicate inspection requirements for qualified manufacturers.

2. **Accelerated MA Pathway:** New marketing authorization fast-track for innovative drugs, orphan drugs, and products addressing urgent public health needs.

3. **Pharmacovigilance Requirements:** Strengthened PV obligations including mandatory local safety reporting and risk management plans.

4. **Tender & Pricing Transparency:** New price negotiation framework for reimbursed drugs, requiring manufacturers to provide reference pricing from originator countries.
