01
Pharmaceuticals Medical Devices Registration

Regulatory Access Services

法规准入服务

Full-process regulatory management covering both pharmaceuticals and medical devices. Our expertise includes device registration, agent certification, import licensing, supplier对接, and distribution. For pharmaceuticals, we handle CTD dossier submission, product certification, pharmacovigilance (PV), risk management planning (RMP), import, and commercial distribution in target markets.

覆盖药品与医疗器械领域的全流程管理，包括医疗器械的注册、代理持证、进口、供应商对接及销售；药品的CTD文件注册提交、产品持证、PV（药物警戒）、RMP（风险管理计划）管理、进口及销售。

02
Orthopedics Cardiovascular Oncology

Medical Device Portfolio

医疗器械产品

Our device focus spans high-value therapeutic areas: orthopedic implants and instruments (spine, trauma, sports medicine), anesthesia devices and consumables, cardiovascular stents and oncology equipment, and blood transfusion products. We provide end-to-end support from regulatory filing to clinical adoption.

重点聚焦骨科植入耗材与工具（含脊柱、创伤、运动医学类）、麻醉科器械与耗材、心血管支架及肿瘤科设备、输血耗材类产品，提供从注册申报到临床推广的全流程支持。

03
GSP-Compliant Distribution Channel Management

Pharmaceutical Marketing & Distribution

药品市场营销

We operate GSP-compliant warehousing facilities and maintain established distribution networks across target markets. Our pharmaceutical commercialization model ensures products reach hospitals and pharmacies efficiently while maintaining full regulatory compliance throughout the supply chain.

配备符合专业GSP标准的仓库及成熟销售渠道，确保药品在合规前提下高效覆盖目标市场的医院及药房终端。

04
Talent Acquisition Compliance Training Medical Affairs

Local Team Building & Clinical Support

本地化团队建设与临床学术支持

We help clients build local regulatory, quality, sales, and medical affairs teams compliant with local requirements. Our full-cycle support covers talent recruitment, compliance training, and daily operations management. Additionally, we organize multi-center clinical studies, real-world data (RWD) collection, physician training, and evidence-based medicine promotion.

协助客户组建符合当地法规要求的注册、质量、销售及医学事务团队，提供从人才招聘、合规培训到日常运营的全周期管理支持；同时组织多中心临床研究、真实世界数据收集、医生培训及循证医学推广。

05
Customs Clearance Cold Chain Last-mile Delivery

Supply Chain & Logistics

供应链与物流协同

Leveraging international logistics resources, we deliver integrated solutions covering customs clearance, warehousing, cold chain transportation, and last-mile delivery to healthcare facilities. Our logistics network ensures product integrity and timely delivery across all target markets.

整合国际物流资源，提供清关、仓储、冷链运输及终端配送一体化解决方案，确保产品在目标市场的完整性与及时交付。

06
Market Research Feasibility Assessment Strategy

Market Intelligence & Consulting

市场咨询服务

We deliver deep market insights through targeted research and go-to-market feasibility assessments. Our analysis covers regulatory entry barriers, competitive landscape, reimbursement systems, clinical practice patterns, and hospital procurement models. Each engagement produces an actionable market entry roadmap with risk mitigation strategies tailored to your product portfolio.

通过定向市场调研与进入可行性评估，提供涵盖准入门槛、竞品格局、支付体系、临床使用习惯及终端采购模式的深度分析，输出可落地的市场进入路线图与风险应对预案。
